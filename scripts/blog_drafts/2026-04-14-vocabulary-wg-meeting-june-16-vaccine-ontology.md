# Vocabulary WG meeting June 16 [Vaccine Ontology]

**Source**: [OHDSI Forums](https://forums.ohdsi.org/t/vocabulary-wg-meeting-june-16-vaccine-ontology/25217)
**Matched keywords**: OHDSI

Please come to the Vocabulary WG meeting on June 16th noon EST (Teams link) where Oliver He, Jie Zheng, and the Vaccine WG will talk about proposed addition of Vaccine Ontology (VO) into the OHDSI Standardized Vocabularies. We will talk about its proposed use for vaccine classification across disparate vocabularies that have vaccines (such as CVX, RxNorm and RxE).
