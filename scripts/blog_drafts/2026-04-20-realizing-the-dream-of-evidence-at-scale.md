# Realizing the dream of evidence at scale

**Source**: [OHDSI Forums](https://forums.ohdsi.org/t/realizing-the-dream-of-evidence-at-scale/25254)

Friends:
Today at the OHDSI Europe Symposium, @RenskeLos and I shared a dream for our community:  to produce reliable evidence at scale, for all medical interventions, for all health outcomes, across all databases around the world.  I asserted that now is the time for us to collaborate to turn this dream into a reality, but also asked the audience to see who shares this dream with us and how can everyone can contribute.
