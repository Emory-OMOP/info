# PCORI Broad Pragmatic Studies — Cycle 2, 2026

PCORI has opened Cycle 2 of its Broad Pragmatic Studies funding announcement, one of the largest mechanisms for patient-centered comparative effectiveness research. Pragmatic studies using observational data infrastructure like OMOP are squarely in scope.

**LOI Deadline**: April 1, 2026 | **Application Deadline**: April 28, 2026

<!-- more -->

## Opportunity details

**LOI Deadline**: April 1, 2026
**Application Deadline**: April 28, 2026
**Type**: Research — Comparative Effectiveness
**Status**: Open

This mechanism funds large-scale pragmatic studies that compare healthcare interventions in real-world settings. OMOP-based infrastructure is a strong foundation for proposals — standardized data across sites, reproducible cohort definitions, and established analytics tooling.
