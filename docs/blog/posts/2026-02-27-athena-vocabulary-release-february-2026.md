# February 2026 Vocabulary Release Available in Athena

OHDSI has published vocabulary release v20260227 in Athena, with a focus on Drug vocabularies. Eleven vocabularies were updated including VANDF/VA Class, OMOP Extension, and Type Concepts.

<!-- more -->

## What's in this release

- **Drug vocabulary overhaul** — major updates to drug-related concept mappings
- **11 vocabularies updated** including VANDF, VA Class, OMOP Extension, and CIEL
- **Type Concept refresh** based on community request
- **EDI vocabulary update** maintained by Korean stewards

## Impact on Emory OMOP

Vocabulary releases affect concept mappings across the pipeline. Teams should review updated drug concepts before pulling the new vocabulary into staging environments. Check for any concept_id changes that may affect active cohort definitions.
