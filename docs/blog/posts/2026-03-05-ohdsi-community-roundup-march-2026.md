# OHDSI Community Roundup — March 2026

A summary of recent OHDSI community activity relevant to Emory's OMOP program, including vocabulary updates, new tooling demos, and collaboration opportunities.

<!-- more -->

## Vocabulary refresh

The [February 2026 Athena release](2026-02-27-athena-vocabulary-release-february-2026.md) (v20260227) landed with major drug vocabulary updates across 11 vocabularies. Teams should review drug concept mappings before pulling the new vocabulary into staging.

## OHDSI Study Agent demo

The OHDSI community previewed a [Study Agent](https://forums.ohdsi.org/t/ohdsi-study-agent-development-demo-with-strategus/25006) that can design, run, and interpret Strategus incidence rate analyses from an R session using natural language. Still in development, but worth watching.

## ML + OMOP discussion

An active thread on [best practices for coupling OMOP with ML metadata](2026-03-02-omop-machine-learning-mlops-best-practices.md) is exploring where model predictions and versioning metadata should live in the CDM.

## MindMeetsMachines at OHDSI EU

The [OHDSI EU Symposium](https://forums.ohdsi.org/) (April 19) will feature a human-vs-AI concept mapping challenge — a practical test of automated vocabulary mapping quality.
