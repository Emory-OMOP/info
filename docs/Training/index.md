# Training

Choose a pathway based on your experience level. The Basic pathway gives you enough to start working with OMOP data; the Advanced pathway builds deeper expertise.

=== ":material-school-outline: Basic Pathway (~1 hour)"

    Foundational concepts for working with the OMOP Common Data Model and the OHDSI ecosystem.

    ???+ example "Step 1 — Real World Data and OMOP *~10 min*"

        - [What is Real World Data, Real World Evidence?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7KX5w_TFNYo&list=PLKudn8e8g-OGNmLatJFfXa8cqubG7cs9H&index=4) — introduction to RWD/RWE in healthcare research and the OMOP-CDM
        - [What is Federated Data and OMOP?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KiemAJwuQjA&list=PLKudn8e8g-OGNmLatJFfXa8cqubG7cs9H&index=3) — how federated data relates to the OMOP CDM

    ???+ example "Step 2 — The Standardized Vocabularies *~45 min*"

        !!! info "Requires a free [EHDEN Academy](https://academy.ehden.eu/my/) account"

        - [Vocabulary Introduction](https://academy.ehden.eu/mod/page/view.php?id=207) — overview of the OMOP Vocabulary and standardized terminology
        - [Concepts](https://academy.ehden.eu/mod/page/view.php?id=208) — how concepts are defined and used in OMOP
        - [Standardized Concepts](https://academy.ehden.eu/mod/page/view.php?id=211) — vocabulary structure and utilization

=== ":material-certificate-outline: Advanced Pathway (~10 hours)"

    Deeper training for researchers ready to build studies and work directly with OMOP infrastructure. We recommend having **database access** so you can apply skills immediately.

    ??? example "1. Data to Evidence with OHDSI"

        [OHDSI2025 Tutorial: Introduction to the Journey from Data to Evidence](https://youtu.be/lELiY5B27ps?si=MbJEGB64mOTsHB7X)

        Foundation for understanding why OHDSI and OMOP matter.

    ??? example "2. CDM and Vocabularies Deep Dive"

        [EHDEN Course: OMOP CDM and Standardized Vocabularies](https://academy.ehden.eu/course/view.php?id=4)

        Detailed breakdown of CDM tables and vocabulary mechanics.

    ??? example "3. ETL Processes"

        - [OHDSI2025 Tutorial: Developing and Evaluating Your ETL Process](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_67Mluiwqc0&list=PLpzbqK7kvfeXRQktX0PV-cRpb3EFA2e7Z&index=1)
        - [EHDEN Course: Extract, Transform and Load](https://academy.ehden.eu/course/view.php?id=7) — supplementary deep dive

    ??? example "4. Vocabularies for Research"

        [OHDSI2025 Tutorial: Using the OHDSI Standardized Vocabularies for Research](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IETB9r4O04I&list=PLpzbqK7kvfeXRQktX0PV-cRpb3EFA2e7Z&index=2)
